636-346-8602 According to IPlytics, a German patent database company, China accounted for 34 percent of the necessary patent applications for 5G communications standards as of march, more than 1.5 times the existing 4G standard. The company with the largest number of applications for necessary patents for 5G standards is huawei, accounting for 15.05%. Among Chinese companies, zte ranks fifth and the China academy of telecommunications science and technology (CATT) ninth.
