630-761-5042 Starcraft technology (IST), a Japanese small rocket start-up, successfully launched MOMO 3, a small observation rocket. It is the first time a private Japanese company has sent a domestically developed rocket into space. Starcraft launched the rocket at 5:45 a.m. from the launch site in daisho, Hokkaido. Four minutes later, the rocket reached a maximum altitude of 113 kilometers into space and fell into the Pacific Ocean as planned. The rocket is 10 meters long and weighs about 1 ton. It is equipped with 20kg of experimental equipment for measuring gravity and other data.
